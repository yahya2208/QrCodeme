QRme - Your Digital Identity Hub

Create a unified digital identity and share all your social profiles, contact information, and links through a single QR code.

KEY FEATURES:

• Digital Identity Creation
Build your personal digital profile with your name, bio, and all your important links in one place.

• Multi-Platform Links
Add links to your social media profiles, websites, phone numbers, email addresses, and more. Supports major platforms including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, Telegram, and many others.

• Secure QR Code Generation
Generate personalized QR codes that others can scan to instantly access your digital identity and connect with you.

• Privacy-Focused Design
You control what information is visible. Your actual links are only revealed when you choose to share them.

• Scan Analytics
Track how many times your QR codes have been scanned to understand your reach and engagement.

• Bilingual Support
Full support for both Arabic (RTL) and English (LTR) interfaces.

• Points & Engagement System
Earn points by sharing the app and engaging with the community. Points reflect your activity level within the platform.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Create your account
2. Build your digital identity with your information and links
3. Share your unique QR code with others
4. Others scan your code to view your profile and connect with you

QRme is perfect for professionals, content creators, business owners, and anyone who wants a modern way to share their contact information and online presence.

Note: Points earned within the app are for engagement purposes only and hold no monetary value.
