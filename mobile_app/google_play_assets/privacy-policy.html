QRme ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

3. How We Use Your Information

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With your consent when you share your QR code publicly

5. Data Storage and Security

  • We use Supabase as our backend provider with enterprise-grade security
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

6. Your Rights
