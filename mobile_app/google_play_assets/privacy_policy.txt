PRIVACY POLICY FOR QRME

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

QRme ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide
- Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
- Profile Information: Name, bio, and links you choose to add to your digital identity
- User Content: QR codes you generate and links you add to your profile

2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device Information: Device type, operating system version
- Usage Data: App interaction patterns, features used, scan counts
- Analytics Data: Anonymized usage statistics to improve our service

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the QRme service
- Create and manage your digital identity
- Generate QR codes for your profile
- Track scan analytics for your QR codes
- Improve and optimize our application
- Communicate service-related updates

4. INFORMATION SHARING

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share information only in these cases:
- With your consent when you share your QR code publicly
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and prevent fraud

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We use Supabase as our backend provider with enterprise-grade security
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data

7. CAMERA PERMISSION

We request camera access solely to scan QR codes within the app. Camera data is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted.

8. STORAGE PERMISSION

Storage permission (Android 12 and below) is used only to save generated QR code images to your device when you choose to download them.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

QRme is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
